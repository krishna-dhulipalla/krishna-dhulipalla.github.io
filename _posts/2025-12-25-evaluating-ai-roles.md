"AI Engineer" title inflation is real.

## The Litmus Test questions

1.  **"Show me your evaluation pipeline."** If they don't have one, they aren't shipping reliable AI.
2.  **"Are you fine-tuning or RAG?"** If they say fine-tuning for everything, they might not understand the cost/benefit.
3.  **"What is your inference latency budget?"** Real products have distinct SLAs.
